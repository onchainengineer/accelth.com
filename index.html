Frequestly Asked Questions

Lets clear out a few things that you might have on your mind.

Is my data safe?

Yes your data is protected using the Fully-Homomorphic Encryption. We only share your data when you give us your consent through our smart consent mechanism.

Are there any hidden charges?

There are no hidden charges, you’ll pay for only for the services you use.

Is my package safe when delivered via drone?

We have industry leading drones having obstacle avoidance, VTOL( Vertical Take Off and Landing) capability, whatever you order reaches the destination safe and sound.

Is telemedicine even feasible?

With the roll-out of 5G the internet is available at low latency and high speed, this means that online consultations just got more real. With the high clarity and easy to access alternative present, why would you travel to the doc’s clinic.

Money back/Refund Policy?

If you book for a consultation offline/online, and the appointment is cancelled by the medical practitioner, you get the full refund no questions asked!
